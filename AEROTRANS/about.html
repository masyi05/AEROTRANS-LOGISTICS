About AeroTrans Logistics Sdn. Bhd.

Your trusted partner in logistics and transportation solutions with over 15 years of industry excellence

Our Company Journey

AeroTrans Logistics Sdn. Bhd. was founded in 2017 with a vision to revolutionize logistics services in Malaysia. What began as a small transportation company has grown into a comprehensive logistics provider serving clients across the nation.

Our logo design symbolizes our three core values: speed, reliability, and professional conduct. The truck represents our extensive ground transportation network, while the airplane signifies our ability to handle time-sensitive, rapid deliveries. The flowing lines illustrate the seamless movement of goods through our optimized supply chain systems.

The deep blue color in our branding represents trust and professionalism, while the grey tones signify stability and reliability. The bold typography reflects our confident approach to solving complex logistics challenges for businesses of all sizes.

Strategic Headquarters

Our central location in Shah Alam, Selangor positions us at the heart of Malaysia's industrial and commercial landscape. This strategic placement provides direct access to major transportation arteries including the Federal Highway, North-South Expressway, and the New Klang Valley Expressway (NKVE) system. Being situated in Malaysia's manufacturing hub allows us to serve industrial clients with unparalleled efficiency.

Location Advantages

  • Direct connectivity to Port Klang and Kuala Lumpur International Airport
  • Proximity to major manufacturing and industrial zones
  • Reduced transit times through optimized route planning
  • Modern warehouse facilities with temperature-controlled storage
  • 24/7 security and advanced inventory management systems
  • Easy access for container trucks and heavy vehicles
